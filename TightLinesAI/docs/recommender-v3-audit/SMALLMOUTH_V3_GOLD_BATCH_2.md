# Smallmouth V3 Gold Batch 2

This is the first real pressure-test batch for smallmouth after Batch 1.

Purpose:
- push harder on stained river movement
- pressure-test fall and winter tightening windows
- force cleaner separation between lake and river SMB behavior
- make sure topwater and baitfish lanes show up when they should, but not when they should not

## Batch Shape

- Scenario count: `10`
- Lake / pond: `5`
- River: `5`
- Clear: `6`
- Stained: `4`

## Pressure Goals

This batch is specifically meant to test:
- colder winter river and lake control
- windy stained spring lake smallmouth
- clear postspawn lake baitfish shifts
- stained low-light summer river SMB behavior
- early-fall and late-fall baitfish tightening

## Review Focus

For each scenario, we should check:
- does the top 1 feel distinctly smallmouth?
- are tubes, hair, Ned, jerkbait, inline spinner, and swimbait lanes showing up in the right windows?
- do rivers feel current-aware and lakes feel rock/clean-water aware?
- are colors realistic for SMB tactics, especially in clear water?
