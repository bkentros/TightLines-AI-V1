# Smallmouth V3 Gold Batch 3

This batch pushes smallmouth into the next layer after the first two gold sets:
- more stained and dirty windows
- more spinnerbait-capable smallmouth scenarios
- harder river flow and visibility adjustments
- late-fall and winter cases where the engine still needs to stay species-correct

## Batch Shape

- Scenario count: `10`
- Lake / pond: `5`
- River: `5`
- Clear: `3`
- Stained: `4`
- Dirty: `3`

## Pressure Goals

This batch is specifically meant to test:
- stained prespawn river smallmouth with spinnerbait lanes
- dirty prespawn and early-winter lake adjustments
- wind-opened stained lake SMB behavior
- late-summer stained lake and river search lanes
- fall baitfish tightening in both lakes and rivers

## Review Focus

For each scenario, we should check:
- does the top 1 still feel clearly smallmouth and not generic bass?
- are spinnerbait lanes showing up when stain, wind, or flow support them?
- do dirty-water recommendations stay visible without drifting into largemouth-only logic?
- do late-fall and winter rows still keep SMB disciplined and lower-column aware?
- are the top color themes realistic for common smallmouth lure and fly usage?
