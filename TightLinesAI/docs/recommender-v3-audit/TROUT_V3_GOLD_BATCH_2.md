# Trout V3 Gold Batch 2

This is the second archived 10-scenario trout truth set.

Purpose:
- pressure-test harder trout river windows
- verify that the archetype pool still feels trout-correct across the U.S.
- stress stained runoff edges, warm-tailwater summer restraint, and late-fall baitfish tightening

## Batch Shape

- Scenario count: `10`
- River: `10`
- Clear: `7`
- Stained: `3`
- Dirty: `0`

This batch stays clear-heavy because trout still punish sloppy presentation and bad color logic most in clearer water, but it adds more stained pressure than Batch 1.

## Hard Windows Covered

- western late-winter control
- spring tailwater generation pulses
- Appalachian spring freestone behavior
- runoff-edge stained western rivers
- clear warm-tailwater midsummer restraint
- late-summer low-water western trout
- Alaska early-fall baitfish and leech windows
- stained fall flow-bump rivers
- late-fall western streamer tightening

## Review Goals

For each scenario, verify:
- the top 1 is something a serious trout angler would start with
- the top 3 stay trout-specific and streamer-minded
- colors remain realistic for trout minnows, buggers, sculpins, and articulated flies
- warm-tailwater summer trout do not over-accelerate
- stained water becomes more visible without turning into bass logic
